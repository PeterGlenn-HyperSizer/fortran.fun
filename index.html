About Phil

Phil Yarrington is an enthusiastic advocate for Fortran, a programming language he finds both fun and powerful. With decades of experience in software development, Phil believes that Fortran remains one of the best tools for scientific and numerical computing.

Why Fortran?

According to Phil, Fortran’s performance and simplicity make it the ideal language for solving complex problems. He loves how Fortran empowers developers to create efficient, elegant solutions that stand the test of time.
